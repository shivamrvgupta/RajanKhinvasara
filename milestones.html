Rajan Khinvasara’s generous financial contributions played a vital role in the expansion of cue sports and firmly positioned India as a leading contributor to the sport's global growth

First Puneite from Maharashtra to be elected to the prestigious post of President of Billiards & Snooker Federation of India (BSFI)

Ex-President of the Billiards & Snooker Federation of India (BSFI)

Ex-President of the Billiards & Snooker Association of Maharashtra (BSAM)

Holder of various positions in the sport’s governing bodies since Year 2008.

Has been instrumental in hosting numerous tournaments at local, State, National, and International levels

Title sponsor of 13 National Championships, 2 Asian championships and 6 World Championships between 2006 and 2020.

Sponsor for five consecutive Indian Opens from 2013 to 2017 and 2019.

Featured in multiple news magazines.

Successfully conducted the 88th Billiards and Snooker Nationals

10th 6 Red Snooker - a 36 days championship in Bhopal in November - December 2021, in spite of then prevalent Covid restrictions.
