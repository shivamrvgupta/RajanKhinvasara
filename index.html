Rajan Khinvasara

Business Mogul - Visionary - Cue Sport Patron

Rajan Khinvasara is the driving force behind the remarkable success of Manisha Constructions, Pune's leading real estate giant, a name synonymous with Residential, Commercial. IT Parks, Malls, Multiplex, Hospitality and Retail.

From projects at prime locations in Pune to award winning projects to glorify, he has brought Manisha Constructions to be a name to be reckoned with in the real estate industry. 

Apart from being a successful businessman, he has assumed the pivotal role of guiding North Star and benefactor to many emerging cue sports enthusiasts. He is not only an accomplished snooker player himself, but also a generous sponsor, a dynamic organizer, a mentor and a visionary administrator of cue sports.

His selfless dedication to leveling the playing field in a sport often perceived to be the pursuit of a privileged few, has garnered deep respect and genuine fondness from the entire cue sports community worldwide
