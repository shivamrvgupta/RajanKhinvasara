“Each one of us has a fire in our hearts to do something. It is our goal in life to find it and keep it lit.”

- Rajan Khinvasara

Rajan's vision is to foster the spirit of entrepreneurship among Indian cue sport players, equipping them with the necessary resources for success and at the same time diverting the spotlight on cue sports worldwide.

This is because he believes that the opportune time for cue sports in India has come. His dream is to see more young Indian cueists shine and establish themselves as a dominant force in the world of cue sports. “It’s high time this sport gets its due respect,” he asserts. He has been working tirelessly to promote and cultivate this sport at various levels, from grassroots to the international stage.

It’s no surprise then, that Rajan commands immense respect within the cue sports fraternity, including players, office bearers, clubs and all State Associations. His unwavering commitment towards propelling the growth and glory of the game forward is truly remarkable.
