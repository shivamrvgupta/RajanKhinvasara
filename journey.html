“From Businessman to Grand Patron of Indian Cue Sports

Rajan Khinvasara has cued his journey through life, navigating the 'breaks' and 'pockets' of challenges and successes.

Stepping into the field of real estate, construction and development establishing his brand “MANISHA” in the year 1985.

He developed his interests in billiards and snooker. Started Promoting sport and helping India shine on the world stage.

The turning point came in the year 2006, when he organized the PCL - MANISHA Open / Invitational Snooker Championship.

In the year 2007 he organized the International Manisha- Vascon World U21 Snooker Championship at GOA.

He has conducted 14 national and 6 international snooker championships under the title sponsorship of Manish Constructions.

The Indian Open was a brainchild of Mr. Khinvasara, a tournament he conceptualized in May 2011 while at Sheffield, England.

Rajan, along with the then President of BFSI, Capt. P.V.K Mohan inaugurated the Indian Open World Ranking Professional Championships in India.
